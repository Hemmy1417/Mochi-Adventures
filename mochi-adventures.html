Combat Manual
How to Play

Mochi is the galaxy's last adorable line of defense. Master the controls, read enemy patterns, and keep those combo chains alive.

Controls

Move Mochi with A / D or Arrow keys.
Press Space to fire Mochi's blaster.
Press P to pause mid-mission.
Use on-screen touch controls on mobile.

Objectives

Clear all aliens in a wave to trigger the Boss.
Defeat the Boss to advance the level.
Chain kills quickly to build Combo multipliers.
Avoid alien shots and body contact.

Enemy Types

Scout — Fast, light, swooping movements.
Spitter — Fires spread shots. Predictable but dangerous.
Tank — 3 HP. Splits into 2 mini-aliens when destroyed.
Diver — Dives toward Mochi. Requires fast reflexes.

Scoring Tips

Kill streaks add +20 bonus points per combo hit.
Boss kills award 900+ bonus points.
Higher difficulty = higher score multiplier.
Save your callsign after a run to hit the board.
