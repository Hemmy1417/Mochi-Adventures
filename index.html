Combat Manual
How to Play

Mochi is the galaxy's last adorable line of defense. Master the controls, read enemy patterns, and keep those combo chains alive.

Controls

Move Mochi with A/D or Arrow keys.
Press Space to fire Mochi's blaster.
Press P to pause mid-mission.
Mobile: D-pad + 🔥 FIRE button on screen.

Objectives

Clear all aliens in a wave to trigger the Boss.
Defeat the Boss to advance to the next level.
Chain kills to build Combo multipliers.
Avoid alien shots and body contact at all costs.

Enemy Types

Scout — Fast, swooping movements.
Spitter — Spread shots. Predictable but deadly.
Tank — 3 HP, splits into 2 mini-aliens.
Diver — Dives straight at Mochi. Reflexes required.

Scoring Tips

Kill streaks add +20 bonus points per combo hit.
Boss kills award 900+ bonus points.
Higher difficulty = higher score multiplier.
Combo chains boost powerup drop frequency!
